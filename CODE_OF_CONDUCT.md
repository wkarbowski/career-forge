# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in the Career Forge project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming language
- Accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments, personal attacks, and other harmful behaviors.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by sending a private message to the maintainer through GitHub. Reports will be reviewed and investigated and will remain confidential.

### Consequences

Violations may result in removal of contributions, temporary or permanent blocking from the project, or other actions deemed necessary by the maintainers.

This code of conduct applies to all interactions in the repository, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and community spaces related to Career Forge.
